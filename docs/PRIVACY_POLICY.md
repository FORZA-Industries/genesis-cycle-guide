# Privacy Policy — Genesyx

**Effective date:** 15 June 2026
**Last updated:** 15 June 2026

Genesyx ("Genesyx," "we," "us," or "our") provides a mobile application that helps
people prepare for conception through cycle awareness, nutrition guidance, and
gentle daily support (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we
collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices and rights you have.

Genesyx handles **health-related information**, which is sensitive. We only collect
what is needed to provide the App's features, we do not sell it, and we do not use
it for advertising.

If you have any questions, contact us at **privacy@genesyx.app** (or
lucasdvsf02626@gmail.com).

---

## 1. Who is responsible for your data

The data controller for the App is the operator of Genesyx. You can reach us at the
contact address above. We use **Supabase** as our backend processor to provide
database, authentication, and hosting services on our behalf (see "Service
providers" below).

## 2. Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to provide by creating an account and using
the App. We do **not** collect location data, contacts, or advertising identifiers,
and the App contains **no ads** and **no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs**.

**a) Account information**
- **Email address** — used to create and secure your account (authentication).
- **Display name** and **profile photo / avatar** — optional, shown in your profile.
- **Account identifiers** — a unique user ID, and, if you link a partner, a
  reference to your partner's account.

**b) Health & wellness information you log** (all optional)
- **Cycle data** — cycle length, period length, and last period date.
- **Daily logs** — mood, energy level, symptoms, sleep duration, water intake,
  supplements taken, and free-text notes.
- **pH readings** — pH values you record, with date/time and optional notes.

**c) Partner invitations** (only if you use partner linking)
- The **email address** you enter to invite a partner, and an invitation code.

We do not knowingly collect any information from the device beyond what is described
here.

## 3. How we use your information

We use your information solely to:
- **Provide the App's core features** — calculate your cycle phase and fertile
  window, show phase-appropriate nutrition and guidance, and store the logs and
  readings you enter.
- **Personalize your experience** — tailor content (e.g., focus foods, insights)
  to where you are in your cycle.
- **Manage your account** — authenticate you, keep you signed in, and let you link
  or unlink a partner.
- **Maintain security and integrity** — prevent unauthorized access and abuse.

We do **not** use your information for advertising or marketing, and we do **not**
sell your personal information.

## 4. Legal basis

Where applicable (e.g., under the GDPR/UK GDPR), we process your information on the
basis of your **consent**, which you provide by creating an account and choosing to
log data, and to **perform our contract** with you in providing the App. You may
withdraw consent at any time by deleting your data or your account (see "Your
rights").

## 5. How your information is shared

- **We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties for their own
  purposes.**
- **Partner linking (your choice):** if you link a partner, that partner can see
  only your **profile information** (display name and avatar). Your **health logs —
  cycle data, daily logs, and pH readings — are never shared** with your partner or
  any other user. Linking and unlinking are controlled by you.
- **Service providers (sub-processors):** we use **Supabase** to host our database,
  authentication, and backend functions. Supabase processes data only to provide
  these services to us, under their terms and privacy commitments.
- **Legal reasons:** we may disclose information if required by law or to protect
  the rights, safety, and security of our users or the App.

## 6. Data security

- All data is **encrypted in transit** using HTTPS/TLS.
- Access is restricted by **row-level security**: your records are accessible only
  to your authenticated account (and, for profile basics only, a partner you
  explicitly link).
- We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your
  information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to
  protect your data appropriately given its sensitivity.

## 7. Data retention and deletion

- We keep your information for as long as your account is active.
- You can **delete your account at any time** from within the App. Deleting your
  account permanently removes your profile, cycle settings, daily logs, pH
  readings, and partner invitations from our systems, and unlinks any partner.
- You may also request deletion by contacting us at the address above.

## 8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:
- **Access** the personal information we hold about you;
- **Correct** inaccurate information (you can edit most data in-app);
- **Delete** your information (via in-app account deletion or by contacting us);
- **Withdraw consent** and stop using the App;
- **Data portability** and to **object to** or **restrict** certain processing.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app controls or contact **privacy@genesyx.app**.

## 9. Children's privacy

Genesyx is intended for adults preparing for conception and is **not directed to
children**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.
If you believe a minor has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

## 10. International data transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country
(by us or Supabase). Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such
transfers.

## 11. Medical disclaimer

Genesyx provides **educational and wellness information only** and is **not a
medical device** and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding fertility, conception,
or any health concern. Do not rely on the App for contraception.

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the
"Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App. Continued
use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
