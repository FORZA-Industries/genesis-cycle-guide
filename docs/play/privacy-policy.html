Privacy Policy — Genesyx

Effective date: 16 June 2026 · Applies to the Genesyx Android app (current version)

Genesyx ("we", "us") provides a mobile application that supports conception preparation through cycle awareness, nutrition guidance, and gentle daily tracking (the "App"). This policy explains our data practices for the current version of the App.

1. No data is collected

The current version of Genesyx works entirely on your device:

  • There are no user accounts and no sign-in.
  • Everything you enter (cycle dates, daily logs, pH readings, notes) is stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information or health information. No data leaves your phone.
  • The App contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking.

2. Deleting your data

Because all data lives on your device, you can remove it at any time by clearing the App's storage in Android settings or uninstalling the App.

3. Children

Genesyx is intended for adults preparing for conception and is not directed to children. The App is rated for users 18 and over.

4. Future versions

A future version of Genesyx may add optional accounts with secure cloud sync (so your data can follow you across devices). If that happens, this policy will be updated before release to describe exactly what is collected, how it is protected, and how you can delete it — and the in-app store listing's Data safety section will be updated to match.

5. Medical disclaimer

Genesyx provides educational wellness information only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Do not rely on the App for contraception. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding fertility or any health concern.
