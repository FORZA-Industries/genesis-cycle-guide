## Full description (≤4000 chars)
```
Feel informed, supported, and ready for your conception journey.

Genesyx is a calm, beautifully designed companion for anyone preparing for
pregnancy. It blends cycle awareness, fertility-focused nutrition, and gentle
daily tracking into one quiet, supportive space — no noise, no pressure.

UNDERSTAND YOUR CYCLE
• See your current cycle phase and predicted fertile window at a glance
• A clear monthly calendar shows period, fertile, and ovulation days
• Set your own cycle length and period length — your rhythm is uniquely yours

EAT FOR YOUR PHASE
• Phase-based nutrition focus that changes as your cycle does
• Simple focus foods for each phase — from iron-rich meals during your period
  to omega-3s around ovulation
• Hydration tracking with a daily goal, plus a simple supplement plan

TRACK HOW YOU FEEL
• Log mood, energy, symptoms, sleep, water, and supplements in seconds
• Add personal notes — a short note for future you
• Track pH readings over time with a clear chart and gentle insights

SEE YOUR PATTERNS
• Insights that turn your logging into understanding: cycle regularity,
  symptom patterns, and nutrition consistency
• Everything is presented gently — information, never judgement

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• Your data stays on your device
• No ads, no third-party trackers
• Designed for adults (18+)

WHAT GENESYX IS NOT
Genesyx is an educational wellness app. It is not a medical device and does not
provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Cycle and fertile-window
estimates are informational and should not be used for contraception. Always
consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding fertility or any health
concern.

Start your journey with a short, personalised quiz — and let Genesyx meet you
where you are.
```
